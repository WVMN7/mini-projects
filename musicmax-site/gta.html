Urban Heist

This is a polished GTA-inspired browser game built in a single file. It includes a city map, driving physics, car hijacking, wanted stars, police chases, checkpoints, money rewards, a minimap, boost, particles, and mission progression.

• Full GTA 5 cannot realistically fit into plain HTML/CSS/JS in one file, so this is a strong top-down prototype.
• Find a car, press E, use WASD to drive, and hit the glowing objective markers.
• Crashing into traffic increases heat. Higher wanted levels spawn more police units.
